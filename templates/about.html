LORE & LEGACY

The deep, completely unserious history of BrainRot Score.

The Origins of the Rot

Legend has it that BrainRot Score was forged in the deepest, darkest trenches of a 3 AM TikTok scroll session. Our scientists realized that humanity was collectively losing its attention span, one Subway Surfers gameplay video at a time. We needed a metric. We needed a diagnosis. We needed... the BrainRot Score.

Is it scientifically accurate?

Absolutely not. Our highly advanced algorithm uses state-of-the-art random number generation intertwined with how deeply you resonate with a dancing doge. If you score over 80, we legally recommend you go outside and touch some grass.
